Goodwill GenAI Referral Assistant
Nava PBC · Nava Labs × Gates Foundation · 2025
GenAI 0→1 Social Impact
Problem
Case workers needed faster, more personalized local service referrals across complex needs.
My Role
End-to-end product lead from kickoff, discovery, PRD, roadmap, pilot launch, and onsite rollout.
Key Decision
Use RAG + eval-driven iteration to improve response quality and speed under tight capacity.
Outcome
Live pilot to 50+ case managers in 4 months with 9x latency improvement.

Led the full product lifecycle for a GenAI-powered decision support tool that helps Goodwill case workers deliver personalized, location-specific social service referrals to clients in need. The tool uses RAG architecture, LLM orchestration via Haystack, and vector databases to generate referrals across housing, food, childcare, transportation, and mental health — parsing intake assessments and conversational inputs.

I joined one week before kickoff, immediately presented to stakeholders, then drove discovery, authored the PRD defining 30+ functional requirements, built the roadmap, and launched a working pilot to 50+ case managers onsite in Austin, Texas in under 4 months. When engineering capacity was tight, I personally ran hundreds of AI evals across dozens of user scenarios using OpenAI Agents, tested LLM models to achieve 9x latency improvement, and built a usage tracking dashboard with cohort analysis using Claude Code.

Massachusetts Childcare Financial Assistance Platform
Nava PBC · MA Executive Office of Education · 2025
Govtech Full-Stack Policy
Problem
Families and administrators were blocked by fragmented legacy systems and policy misalignment.
My Role
Product strategy lead for modernization vision, scope, roadmap, requirements, and stakeholder management.
Key Decision
Create durable product brief and phased roadmap to align delivery with policy complexity.
Outcome
6-month contract delivered on time with a path to replace 3 systems statewide.

Led product strategy for a ground-up modernization of Massachusetts' childcare financial assistance (CCFA) program — replacing fragmented legacy systems (KinderWait, FACTS, CCFA-the-software) that required extensive manual data entry and didn't reflect current policy. The new platform unifies the application, waitlist, and eligibility process for families in need across the state.

Drove the entire product lifecycle: led a stakeholder visioning workshop to align competing perspectives, authored the product brief setting vision, strategy, and scope boundaries, then built a phased 6-month roadmap with 9-month contingency. Wrote 100+ product requirements and presented weekly to EEC leadership. When the work created design-engineering bottlenecks, I led difficult conversations to rework the delivery process and keep velocity up. Onboarded and trained a senior PdM to carry the work forward.

CDC ReportStream — Public Health Data Infrastructure
Nava PBC · Centers for Disease Control · 2022–2024
Public Health At Scale Federal
Problem
Public health agencies needed reliable, scalable routing for critical lab and case-report data nationwide.
My Role
Sole technical PM for the Universal Pipeline team managing roadmap, priorities, and stakeholder alignment.
Key Decision
Drive platform scaling, standards translation, and new data ingestion capabilities in parallel.
Outcome
10x throughput scaling, new data markets, and a leadership-facing metrics dashboard.

Served as the sole technical product manager for the Universal Pipeline team on ReportStream — the CDC's core data routing engine that delivers millions of public health messages (lab results, case reports, test orders) to state and local agencies nationwide. Managed the team's roadmap, sprint planning, stakeholder relationships, and technical vision for 2+ years.

Led initiatives spanning code-to-condition automation, 10x message throughput scaling, microservices architecture, HL7-to-FHIR translation mappings (most comprehensive in the industry), cloud infrastructure load testing, and a product metrics dashboard in Power BI now used across CDC leadership. Opened new data markets (OTC via MakeMyTestCount.org, ETOR, CSV-to-FHIR ingestion) and drove a partnership expansion with Indian Health Services advancing CDC health equity goals. Created a research playbook for evaluating new public health data types. Trained two replacement PMs and contributed to multiple contract bids including winning an expanded cloud management scope.
